Experimental Chrome API

Drop Everything

A bookmarklet that turns any webpage into a physics playground. Every word becomes a solid body that falls and bounces, rendered with the new HTML-in-Canvas API.

Drop Everything

Drag the button above to your bookmarks bar.

  1. Enable chrome://flags/#canvas-draw-element in Chrome Canary
  2. Drag the Drop Everything button to your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to any webpage (Wikipedia, news sites, etc.)
  4. Click the bookmarklet and watch everything fall
Requires Chrome Canary with chrome://flags/#canvas-draw-element enabled. The bookmarklet loads drop.js from this server.
